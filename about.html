About Open Ideas

This website is a model of Open Ideas. A place for Open Business. It's an experiment of what could be an online hub to help Non-profit or Social Businesses to share their knowledge.

It's also a place to copy positive business locally. Take ideas, improve them and bring back your experience.

Finally, a place to document what was did. For internal purpose but also to receive external feedback or help similar projects.

Nothing here is final (starting with the name ;-). If you like this project please signup!
